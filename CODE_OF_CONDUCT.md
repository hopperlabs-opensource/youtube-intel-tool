# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

We are committed to a respectful, constructive environment.

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Giving and receiving constructive feedback
- Taking responsibility for mistakes and learning from them
- Focusing on what is best for the project and community

Examples of unacceptable behavior:
- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination
- Insults, personal attacks, or trolling
- Publishing private information without consent
- Any conduct that is inappropriate in a professional setting

## Scope

This policy applies in all project spaces, including issues, pull requests,
discussions, and other official communication channels.

## Enforcement

Report incidents to project maintainers via private channels where possible.
Maintainers are responsible for investigating reports and enforcing this policy.
